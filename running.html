"Running Man" Title Sequence

Here I created an opening sequence for a popular South Korean variety show called "Running Man." There is no real title sequence for this show. I took objects that are known to represent each member of the show and used Art Deco as my inspiration to make a more vibrant and fun title sequence

Running Man Opening Sequence Redesign from Kim Duong on Vimeo.
